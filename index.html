OUTREACH SCHOOL

Outreach School, located in the heart of Wilson Garden, Bangalore, is a vibrant learning community that follows the ICSE curriculum.
Established in 2005, the school has been nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders for over 15 years. 1 With a focus on holistic development, Outreach School provides a well-rounded education that goes beyond textbooks and exams. The school's commitment to individual attention, experienced faculty, and a wide range of extracurricular activities creates a stimulating environment where students can explore their interests, discover their talents, and grow into confident, responsible individuals.
